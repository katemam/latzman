About

Your Independent Partner for Engagement with Swiss Financial Institutions

Latzman Advisors AG is a Zurich-based advisory firm supporting international clients, family offices and professional advisors in their engagement with Swiss financial institutions.

The firm combines compliance expertise with practical knowledge of Swiss financial market expectations, providing advisory support, compliance preparation and coordination throughout institutional onboarding processes.
